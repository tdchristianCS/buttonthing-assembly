How To Play

Press the correct keys when the notes reach the reach the rectangular area. The more accurate you are, the more points you get.

Controls:

Tap notes: DFJK

Start Game: ENTER

Adjust Size: Command +/- for MacOS, Control +/- for Windows

Point Distribution:

10 points per note. No points if note missed. Getting accurate timings increase your combo, and getting innacurate timings will reset the combo. Combo of 10 or higher will up score gain to 20 per note, and combo of 50 or higher will up point gain to 30 per note.

Note Timing

You may notice sometimes the notes don't align with the beat. This is to introduce randomness and have more to the gameplay than memorization. Yup, let's go with that. It's a feature now. Totally not a bug.
