Funni Buttons

How To Play

Press the correct keys when the notes reach the reach the rectangular area. The more accurate you are, the more points you get.

Controls

Tap notes: DFJK

Start Game: ENTER

Stop Game: ESCAPE

Adjust Size: Command +/- for Mac, Control +/- for Windows

Point Distribution

10 points per note. No points if note missed. Getting accurate timings increases your combo, and getting inaccurate timings will reset the combo. Combos of 10 or higher will increase score gain to 20 per note, and combo of 50 or higher will increase point gain to 30 per note.

Note Timing

You may notice sometimes the notes don't align with the beat. This is to introduce randomness and have more to the gameplay than memorization. Yup, let's go with that. It's a feature now. Totally not a bug.
